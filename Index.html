Header Banner

The header banner is a prominent visual element at the top of this webpage, serving as a key focal point that sets the tone for the site.

It features a striking image that aligns with the website's theme and a headline that introduces the website's purpose



Central (hero) Banner

A central or hero banner is an eye-catching section that serves as one of the main visual and messaging focal point of a website, immediately capturing visitor's attention.

It delivers a compeling message that clearly conveys the site's purpose or value, prompting users to take action, explore, and stay engaged.



Footer Banner

A footer banner is a visual section located at the bottom of a webpage, often used to reinforce key messages, provide additional navigation,

or encourage user engagement before they leave the site.
